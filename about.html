Our Mission

SomBazar was built to give every person in Somaliland a safe, simple, and powerful platform to buy and sell anything — from cars and electronics to real estate and services. We believe local commerce should be easy, trusted, and accessible to all.

What We Offer

Free listings for everyone. Verified seller badges. Secure offer negotiation between buyers and sellers. Affordable subscription plans for businesses and power sellers. Mobile-first design built for Somaliland's internet users.

Our Values

Trust: Every listing is reviewed. Every seller can be verified. Reports are handled within 2 hours.

Simplicity: Post an ad in under 2 minutes. No complicated forms or fees to get started.

Community: We are built by and for the people of Somaliland.

Headquarters

Hargeisa, Somaliland. Our team operates Saturday through Thursday, 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (EAT).
