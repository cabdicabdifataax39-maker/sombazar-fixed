1What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and keep you logged in between sessions. SomBazar uses a combination of browser cookies and local storage to provide a better experience.

SomBazar does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. We only use what is strictly necessary to operate the platform.

3What We Do NOT Use

SomBazar does not use:

  • Google Analytics or any behavioral analytics tracking
  • Facebook Pixel or social media tracking
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Cross-site tracking technologies
  • Fingerprinting or device identification beyond session management
We do not track your activity on other websites. We only collect data about your activity on SomBazar itself.

4Local Storage

In addition to cookies, SomBazar uses browser Local Storage to save data on your device. This includes:

  • Your authentication token for staying logged in
  • Draft listing data while you're composing a new ad
  • UI preferences such as selected language and color theme

Local storage data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to third parties.

5How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Opera: Settings → Advanced → Privacy & security → Site settings
Disabling essential cookies (sb_token) will log you out and prevent you from posting listings or sending messages. Functional cookies are optional but improve your experience.

6Clearing Your Data

To clear all SomBazar cookies and local storage from your device:

  • Log out of your account (this clears your session token)
  • Or clear site data for sombazar.com from your browser settings
  • Or delete your account entirely from Profile → Settings → Delete Account

7Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add new features that require additional storage. We will notify you of meaningful changes by displaying a notice on the platform. The "Last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.
