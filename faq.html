How do I post a listing?

Click "Post Ad" in the navigation bar. You must be logged in. Fill in the details, upload at least one photo, and submit. Your listing will be reviewed and published within 2 hours.

How much does it cost to list?

Basic listings are free (up to 3 listings). For more listings and features, upgrade to Standard ($8/mo), Pro ($20/mo), or Agency ($50/mo).

How do I pay for a package?

Go to your Profile → Payments. Send payment via EVC Plus or Zaad, then submit your reference code and screenshot. Our team verifies and activates your plan within 2–4 hours.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. Your password is encrypted. Messages are encrypted. We never sell your data. Photos have GPS data removed automatically.

How do I report a scam?

Click the Report button on any listing or user profile. Select "Fraud/Scam" and describe the issue. We investigate all reports.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your account enters a 30-day recovery window before permanent deletion. You can recover it anytime within 30 days.

What payment methods does SomBazar accept?

For seller packages: EVC Plus and Zaad. For transactions between buyers and sellers: that is between you and the seller directly — SomBazar does not process those payments.

How do I verify my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Get Verified. Upload a photo of your national ID (front and back) and a selfie holding your ID. Verification is usually complete within 24 hours.
