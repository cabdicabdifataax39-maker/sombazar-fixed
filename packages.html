Frequently Asked Questions
How long does activation take? ▼
We manually verify every payment. Activation takes up to 2 hours during business hours (8am–8pm). You'll see your plan update in your profile once approved.
What happens when my plan expires? ▼
Your account reverts to the Free plan. Your existing active listings will remain visible until they expire naturally (30 days), but you won't be able to post new ones beyond the free limit of 3.
Can I upgrade mid-plan? ▼
Yes. Submit a new payment for a higher plan and contact us via WhatsApp with your reference code. We will upgrade your plan immediately upon verification.
Are refunds available? ▼
Payments are non-refundable once a plan is activated. If payment was not approved after 4 hours, contact us via WhatsApp and we will investigate.
