1Overview

SomBazar is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it when you use our platform.

This policy applies to all users of SomBazar — buyers, sellers, businesses, and visitors. By using SomBazar, you consent to the data practices described here.

We do not sell your personal data to anyone. Your information is used solely to provide, operate, and improve SomBazar's services.

We do not collect sensitive categories of personal data such as ethnic origin, religious beliefs, political views, or biometric data.

3How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for these purposes:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Displaying and managing your listings
  • Enabling secure messaging between buyers and sellers
  • Processing and verifying mobile money payments
  • Sending transactional emails (welcome, password reset, listing status)
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, scams, and prohibited content
  • Improving platform performance and user experience
  • Complying with Somaliland legal obligations
  • Responding to customer support inquiries
We do not use your data for advertising profiling, political targeting, or any purpose not listed above.

4Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Data is shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Other users: Your display name, city, and listings are visible to users. Your email and phone are never shown unless you include them in your listing.
  • Email service: We use Gmail SMTP to send transactional emails. Google does not receive your full profile data.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data if required by Somaliland law enforcement, court order, or to prevent serious harm.
  • Safety: We may share limited information to investigate fraud or protect user safety.
We will never share your data with advertisers, data brokers, insurance companies, or political organizations.

5Data Security

We implement strong technical measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords: Stored as bcrypt hashes — never stored in plain text
  • Messages: Encrypted with AES-256-CBC before storing in database
  • Sessions: JWT tokens with automatic expiration and invalidation
  • File uploads: Stored with randomized UUID filenames, type-verified
  • Transport: All data transmitted over HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
  • Admin access: Protected by CSRF tokens and admin-only authentication
  • Rate limiting: Login attempts limited to prevent brute-force attacks

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your account, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery.

6Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account, then removed within 30 days
  • Active listings: Kept while your account is active
  • Deleted listings: Permanently removed within 90 days of deletion
  • Messages: Stored for 2 years, then automatically deleted
  • Payment records: Retained for 5 years for financial compliance purposes
  • Verification documents: Deleted within 30 days of verification decision
  • Usage logs: Anonymized after 12 months

7Your Rights

You have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate data in Profile → Settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Data portability: Request an export of your data in a standard format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any right, contact privacy@sombazar.com. We respond within 14 days.

8Cookies

SomBazar uses essential cookies and browser local storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (language, dark mode). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. See our full Cookie Policy for details.

9Children's Privacy

SomBazar is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child under 18 has registered, contact us at support@sombazar.com and we will delete the account immediately.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you by email and display a notice on the platform at least 7 days before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision.
