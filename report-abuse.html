2What Happens After You Report

  • Within 2 hours: High-priority reports (fraud, weapons, harassment) are reviewed
  • Within 24 hours: All other reports are reviewed by our moderation team
  • Action taken: Listing removed, user warned, or account banned depending on severity
  • Repeat offenders: Accounts with multiple violations are permanently banned
  • Serious crimes: Cases involving weapons, drugs, or human trafficking are reported to Somaliland Police
All reports are treated confidentially. We will never reveal your identity to the reported party.

3Emergency Contact

For urgent safety concerns — threats, violence, or criminal activity — do not wait for our review. Contact us directly:

  • Email: abuse@sombazar.com
  • WhatsApp: +252 63 XXX XXXX (urgent reports only)
  • Somaliland Police: 999
If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact Somaliland Police (999) immediately. Do not wait for our response.

4False Reports

Submitting false or malicious reports against other users is a violation of our Terms of Service and may result in your own account being suspended. Please only report genuine violations.
