✓ Always Meet in Public

For in-person transactions, always meet in a public place — like a busy café, market, or bank. Never meet at someone's home or in a private location.

✓ Verify Before Paying

Inspect the item in person before paying. For vehicles, check registration documents. For property, verify ownership documents with a lawyer.

✓ Use Verified Sellers

Look for the ✓ Verified badge on seller profiles. Verified sellers have confirmed their identity with us.

!️ Never Pay in Advance

Never send money before seeing the item. Legitimate sellers do not ask for deposits or advance payments for basic items.

!️ Beware of Unrealistic Prices

If a price seems too good to be true, it probably is. Scammers often advertise cars or phones at very low prices to attract victims.

!️ Never Share Personal Financial Info

Never share your bank account details, EVC PIN, or Zaad credentials with anyone on the platform.

! Report Fraud Immediately

If you suspect fraud, report the listing immediately using the Report button. We investigate all reports within 24 hours.

! Do Not Pay via Western Union

SomBazar will never ask you to pay via Western Union, MoneyGram, or cryptocurrency. These are common scam payment methods.
