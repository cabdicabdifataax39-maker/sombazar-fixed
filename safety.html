What defines a violation?
Reporting the right category ensures faster investigation and a cleaner marketplace for everyone.
warning
Fraud & Scams
Fake listings, advance-fee fraud, phishing attempts, counterfeit goods, or misleading pricing and item descriptions.
block
Prohibited Items
Illegal goods, weapons, drugs, stolen property, or any items that violate Somaliland law or our marketplace policies.
person_off
Harassment
Offensive or threatening messages, bullying, discrimination of any person using the SomBazar platform.
content_copy
Duplicate Listings
Spam listings, the same item posted multiple times, or artificial inflation of listings to game the search ranking.
manage_accounts
Fake Accounts
Impersonating another person or business, using stolen identity, or creating multiple accounts to evade bans.
image_not_supported
Wrong Category
Items listed in the wrong category, misleading titles, or descriptions that don't match what is being sold.
