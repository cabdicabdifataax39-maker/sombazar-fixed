7Transactions & Safety

SomBazar strongly recommends the following safety practices for all transactions:

  • Meet in person in a public place — markets, shops, or busy areas in Hargeisa
  • Inspect items thoroughly before paying
  • Never send money in advance to someone you have not met
  • Be suspicious of prices that seem too good to be true
  • Do not share personal banking or mobile money PIN with anyone
  • Check the seller's verification badge and reviews before transacting

SomBazar is not responsible for the outcome of transactions between users. We are not a bank, escrow agent, or guarantor.
